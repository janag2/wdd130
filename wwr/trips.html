Half-Day Rafting Trip with Credd Rafting

Trip Details
- Duration: 4 hours (half-day)
- Distance: Approximately 6 miles of river navigation
- Difficulty: Moderate (Class II-III rapids)
- Time: Morning (9:00 am - 1:00 pm) or Afternoon (2:00 pm - 6:00 pm)
- Ages: 8 years old and above
- Weight: 100 pounds (45 kg) minimum

The Adventure Begins
Your half-day rafting trip with Credd Rafting starts with a brief introduction and safety orientation at our riverside base. Our experienced guides will provide you with a comprehensive overview of the trip, including safety procedures, paddling techniques, and what to expect on the river. Before hitting the water, you'll receive a thorough gear briefing and be fitted with a properly sized life jacket (PFD), helmet a paddle, water shoes or sandals with a secure strap and a dry bag or waterproof container for your belongings. As you climb aboard the raft, our guides will provide a final safety check and brief you on your role in navigating the river. You'll then embark on an exciting journey down the river, taking in the stunning scenery and enjoying the thrill of navigating Class II-III rapids. Throughout the trip, you'll encounter a mix of exciting rapids and scenic floats. Our guides will expertly navigate the raft through the rapids, providing you with an exhilarating experience while ensuring your safety. During the calmer stretches, you'll have the opportunity to take in the breathtaking scenery and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of the river. To keep your energy levels up, we'll provide snacks and refreshments during the trip. You'll have the chance to enjoy some delicious treats and rehydrate while taking in the stunning views. After an exciting 4 hours on the water, you'll return to our riverside base, where you can take some time to relax and unwind. Our guides will provide a final briefing, and you'll have the opportunity to ask questions and share your experiences.
