Credd Rafting

At Credd Rafting, we're committed to:

  • Providing unforgettable white water rafting experiences that inspire a sense of adventure and connection with nature
  • Maintaining the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and environmental responsibility
  • Building a supportive community of rafting enthusiasts and promoting the sport's growth and development

History

At Credd Rafting, we're passionate about sharing the thrill of water rafting with our guests. Founded in 2024 by Jesse A., our company is built on a foundation of adventure, safety, and community. Jesse's love affair with water rafting began as a guide on a local river. As his passion grew, so did his vision for a rafting company that would prioritize exceptional guest experiences, environmental stewardship, and community engagement. In 2024, Jesse turned his vision into reality, launching Credd Rafting with a small team of dedicated guides and staff. From the outset, our focus has been on providing high-quality, guided rafting trips that cater to a wide range of ages and skill levels.
